We're in a huge revolution

Did you notice we’re in the middle of a huge revolution? Unmanaged life evolved slowly, through copying, mutation, succeeding, failing. These days we’ve entered the era of digital life in which generations of technology follow each other much more rapidly. Is this wholly different from the way life changed before?

τὰ πάντα ῥεῖ καὶ οὐδὲν μένει
Change is the only constant factor

Heraclitus (540 - 480 BC)
Not really, Heraklitus already stated his famous *pantha rei kai ouden menei*, everything flows, nothing is permanent. Change is the only constant factor in the history of our universe. There is however, a noticeable difference in the frequency of change.

Mapping technological change

We experience life through the rearview mirror, we’re walking backwards into the future.

Understanding Media by Marshall McLuhan
Mapping technological change is the process of trying to make sense of the current wave we're riding. Because the future is by definition unknowledgable we have to look back, learn from experiences we've and extrapolate onto possible futures.
